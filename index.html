Cuphead's gameplay is centered on continual boss fights, interspersed with run-and-gun levels. Each is housed in one of four worlds, with the final fight against the Devil.

GAME DEVELOPMENT

Cuphead is the first game by Studio MDHR, a Canadian indie game development studio consisting of brothers Chad and Jared Moldenhauer. It was inspired by cartoons from the early days of golden age of American animation such as Disney and Fleischer Studios, along with cartoonists Ub Iwerks, Grim Natwick, and Willard Bowsky.
